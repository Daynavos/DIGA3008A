About Me

Hi, I'm Dayna Vos

I'm based in Johannesburg, South Africa, and currently a third-year BEngSc Digital Arts student at Wits University That means I spend my days making games, and my nights wrangling multivariable calculus (or sometimes the other way around). I love making things; games, songs, drawings, stories, even the occasional piece of woodworking. Creation is at the core of who I am. This site is a collection of some of the things I’ve made. Feel free to explore and if you'd like, you can maybe discover a new indie artist with the random indie song generator.
